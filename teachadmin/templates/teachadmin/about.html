Mission

The main mission of TeachAdmin is to help all teachers to manage their students through data.
This world has gone way too far without taking real life data into account when making critical decisions. Teachers are faced with piles of decisions due to changing circumstances their students are in.
To always be on the frontline of personal progress requires insight into ALL students - not just the ones the teacher happen to connect with exceptionally well. By automatically providing graphs for individual kids and whole groups/homerooms, teachers do not have to keep their own Google SpreadSheets to generate the data they need anymore. All they have to do now is to sipmly add the scores into TeachAdmin and then analyze the graphs that pop up. While the long-term goal is to be able to predict certain behavior/events and thus helping the teacher out in helping each individual student out, the starting point is simply going to be displaying graphs that are useful for the teacher to improve their students' results.
